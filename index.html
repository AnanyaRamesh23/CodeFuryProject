Common Scams & Red Flags

Phishing Emails

Beware of emails asking for personal details, OTPs, or banking info.

  • ✅ Check sender’s email carefully
  • ✅ Never click unknown links
  • ✅ Banks never ask for OTPs

Fake UPI Payment Requests

Fraudsters may send a “request money” link instead of paying you.
